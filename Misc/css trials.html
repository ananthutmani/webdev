Doom 2016

Synopsis

Doom is a run and shoot action game released by id Tech in 2016 to commemmorate the 25th anniversary of the popular 90's retro game DOOM.

It consists of several 18+ elements like:

  • Blood and gore
  • Splattered guts
  • Heavy metal music that will rip out your eardrums.
  • little toy souveniers that make no sense at all considering the intense gameplay.

Overall, its a very soothing experience.
